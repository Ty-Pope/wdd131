Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create, read, and update a single elimination bracket. You will input your team names and rank and a bracket will be generated for you.

Audience

The audience for this site is anyone who needs to generate a single elimination bracket (like for a tournament.) My (real) audience is my professor and TA who are grading this final project.

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements will be the bracket itself. The bracket will be generated using javascript.
