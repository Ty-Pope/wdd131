What is a single elimination bracket?

A single-elimination, knockout, or sudden death tournament is a type of elimination tournament where the loser of each match-up is immediately eliminated from the tournament. The losing team or player is eliminated from the competition, while the winner advances to the next round. This process continues until only one team or player remains, who is declared the champion.
